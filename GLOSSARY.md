# Glossary

* Added Attractions (addon): musi sin
* Company Value (the monetary value of your company): mani lawa
* Excitement Value: mani pi pilin wawa
* Intensity Value: mani pi wawa sijelo
* Loopy Landscapes: ma tawa sike
* Options: lawa (pi ...)
* Park: ma, ma musi
* Park Rating (the ingame score of how good your park is): mani ma
* Park Value (the monetary value of your park): mani tomo
* Rail (as in the track on which a ride/rollercoaster train is on): nasin, nasin palisa, nasin kiwen, nasin kasi
* RollerCoaster/Ride: ilo tawa musi
* RollerCoaster Tycoon (the game): musi pali pi ilo tawa musi
* RollerCoaster Tycoon 2 (the game): musi nanpa tu, musi pali nanpa tu pi ilo tawa musi
* Scenario: tenpo
* Server: ilo weka
* Time Twister: tenpo tawa sike
* Toolbar: ilo linja
* Wacky Worlds (addon): ma nasa
* Window: lipu
